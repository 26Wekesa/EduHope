Connecting Learners, Sponsors, Mentors, and Schools

EduHope Portal is a digital platform designed to connect learners in need with sponsors, mentors, and schools in an efficient, transparent, and secure way. We support children—especially boys and vulnerable learners—who face financial, social, or educational barriers by giving them a space where their stories, talents, and academic profiles can be shared with well-wishers ready to help.

About EduHope Portal

EduHope Portal connects learners in need with sponsors, mentors, and schools. Our mission is to break barriers and create pathways to opportunity, hope, and a brighter future for every child.

Empowering Youth Through Education

Verified students can be registered by teachers, guardians, or community leaders, allowing sponsors to view profiles, confirm authenticity, and offer educational support. Schools and organizations can book mentorship sessions, engage volunteers, and collaborate for youth development.

Mentors can apply for school visits, and schools can request sessions tailored to leadership, mental health, boy-child empowerment, digital literacy, and talent development. Our executive admin dashboard enables B.O.L.D Foundation team members to manage users, verify profiles, and maintain security and integrity.

EduHope Portal strengthens partnerships, elevates youth voices, and ensures every deserving learner has access to opportunity, guidance, and hope for a brighter future.
