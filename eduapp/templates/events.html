Community Event 2024

Chapati Festival Fundraiser

A community-driven event dedicated to feeding, empowering, and supporting children through charity, mentorship, and engagement.

Event Narrative

The Chapati Festival is an annual charity event organized by B.OLD Foundation to support underserved communities. The event brings together volunteers, parents, youth, and well-wishers to make chapati, distribute food, donate clothes, and mentor children.

This event is a pillar of our mission to empower the boy child by addressing emotional, social, and physical needs through direct community involvement.
